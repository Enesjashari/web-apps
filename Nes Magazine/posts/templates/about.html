Company History

Nes Magazine is an online portal/magazine founded in December 2022 by Enes Jashari. The work to create Nes Magazine began in February 2022. The founder, Enes Jashari, is an entrepreneur, programmer, and enthusiast of Artificial Intelligence, who decided to create an online platform that would provide the latest news, reviews, and information about the AI and ML updates for community of AI enthusiasts.

Since its launch, Nes Magazine has quickly established itself as a leading source of information and entertainment for News enthusiasts. The portal has gained a large and dedicated readership, and has been praised for its high-quality content and commitment to providing accurate and informative coverage of community.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

The main aim of Nes Magazine is to provide in-depth coverage of the latest trends and developments in the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This includes news on the latest research and developments, reviews of new AI and ML products and services, and interviews with industry experts. The portal also provides a platform for AI enthusiasts to connect and share their knowledge and experiences with each other.

Nes Magazine's team is constantly working to improve the portal and to add new features that will make it even more valuable to its readers. The portal's mission is to connect enthusiasts around the world and make the community even stronger. And with the constant updates and new features, Nes Magazine will continue to be the leading source of information and entertainment for it's enthusiasts.
